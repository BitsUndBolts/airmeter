Installer
v1.0

Requires a Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera) with Web Serial support. Firefox and Safari are not supported.

Before you begin
1
Connect your ESP32-C3 SuperMini to this computer with a USB data cable.
2
Click Install Firmware below and select the correct serial port when prompted.
3
Wait for the flash to complete, then connect to the AirMeter-Setup Wi-Fi hotspot to configure your network.
Flash Firmware
Your browser does not support Web Serial.
Please use Chrome, Edge, or another Chromium-based browser.
